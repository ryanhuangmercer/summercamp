Summer Camp China - Beijing 2010

Realizing your dreams in Beijing - Learning Chinese in China

Where can you study, travel, learn, discover, and explore a new culture? You can do it all at the Beijing GoldenDream summer program. For two or four weeks, students will not only study Chinese language and culture but also take a magnificent and eye opening tour of China. GoldenDream has been offering summer camps programs for the past five years. We have assembled the best faculty and programs offering multiple theme summer camps for kids aging from 6 to 18.
