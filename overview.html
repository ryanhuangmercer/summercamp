GoldenDream Summer Camp Overview

Realizing your dreams in Beijing - Learning Chinese in China

Looking for a summer camp for your kids? Want to learn Chinese in China?

Camp Information:

GoldenDream summer camp is accepting applications from students aging from 6 to 18. No prior Chinese language skill required. Our program consists a variety of courses and activities to make the camp educational and fun! One of the camp is located at the foot of beautiful xiang-shan mountain - ShangLi Foreign Language School and another camp is located at LianHua Academy(WangFu Garden) *.

Application process:

Admission to our program is competitive and space is limited, so we strongly encourage students to complete the application as soon as possible. The deadline for applying to our program is May 31st. Late applicants will be evaluated only on a case by case basis. A complete application includes the signed application form, signed terms and agreement, and $100 deposit.
