Realizing your dreams in Beijing - Learning Chinese in China

As the capital city of China, Beijing is the nation's political, economic, cultural and educational center.In 2008, Beijing will host the summer Olympic game first time in China! Our students will visit famous places in or around Beijing such as Great Wall, Tiananmen Square, National Museum, Forbidden City, Zoo, Puppet show, Fragrant Hill Park, Peking University, Zhongguan Village and the Olympic village etc.

Oh, the Great Wall of China! "Those who didn't go to the Great Wall aren't real heros" A place we will go :-)

Tian'an men Square is the largest one all over the world. A place full of history and events. China 's national museum is based on the China 's historic museum and revolutionary museum. Yuanmingyuan is renowned throughout the world for its fabled charms and association with Chinese modern history. The Forbidden City is the most comprehensive Chinese museum. It was the ritual center of two dynasties, the Ming and the Qing.
 The Summer palace lies in the west of BeiJing surburb , representing the royal and wealth of Middle Kingdom in Qing dynasty.
The Temple of Heaven was the place where the Emperors of the Ming and Qing dynasties would worship Heaven and pray for bumper crops. The China Puppet Show Troupe was founded in 1955. For more than 40 years since its inception, the troupe has produced over 300 performances. Beijing People's Art Theatre, a first-rate national theatre company of China, its development in the last 50 years has been a miniature of the development of Chinese modern theatre. Peking University Hall, an international-standard 2100-seat theatre, is an ideal venue for the Series, with good sightlines and high-quality sound. Beijing Zoo hosts rare Chinese animals, such as the Giant Panda and also a strong selection from around the world - from Polar Bears to Kangaroos. The 400-hectare Beijing Botanical Garden is located at the foot of the Western Hills. Along with a large variety of plants, the garden has Asia's largest greenhouse.
Fragrant Hills Park, the site of several Ming and Qing dynasty temples and villas. It has spectacular views from its high peak. Quanjude roast duck enjoys a high reputation among domestic and overseas consumers for the peculiar roast technique and outstanding quality.

The Hu Guo Si was constructed in Yuan Dynasty. In those old times, there were many great snack shops around that area.

 The Temple of Confucius was initially built in 1302. It is constructed for Confucius, the greatest thinker and educationalist in ancient China. The Imperial College is recognized as the highest official institution of learning in imperial China, it was established in 1287 during the Yuan Dynasty. Ba Da Chu Park is a large park in the western hills of Beijing, about 8 kilometers south of XiangShan Park and about 25 kilometers from central Beijing.
Ling Guan temple worships a tooth of Gotama Buddha's for over one thousand and five handred years. Zhongguan Village, China's silicon valley. Higher education institute and high tech center. Olympic village, the place to host people from the world for the 2008 Olympic Summer Games. The PRC Science and High-Tech Center , it has 800,000, 000 visitors every year and showcase technology advancement of China. Peking University is one of top universities in China . It was founded as Capital College in 1898. It is famous for its literature and arts. Tsinghua University was established in 1911 on the site of "Qing Hua Yuan" and is one of China's top universities in technology.
